Process

1. Pages

Before starting any work on the website, I first planned the pages I wanted to have on it. I chose four main pages: home, about me, travel and hobbies. I decided to have under the travel page a blog about my travel experience and to have under the hobbies page two other pages named photography and art.

2. Logo

I created different logos with various font types and images. However, none were of my linking. I also created different variations of a single logo and ended up with the logo presented on my website, which I felt like it represented me.

3. Colour of the Website

I took some time to choose the colours of my website because I did not have a preference when I started this project. So, I tried different colour. At first, I chose a dark shade of red, which I did not like. Then, I decided to change the colour to the current shade of blue.

4. Fonts

I did not have a preference for fonts when I started this project. So, I used FontJoy as a tool to choose my fonts. I ended up picking the ones that matched the style of my website.

5. Creating the website

I did not create wireframes for my pages because I did not know how to organize each page. So, what I did was try different things and styles and leave everything that I thought would work on my website.
