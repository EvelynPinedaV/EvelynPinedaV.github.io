Hello!

I'm a young adventurer who enjoys travelling to new places and blogging my adventures on Instagram.

Currently residing in Canada, my travel credentials include 29 countries visited over the past eleven years.

Exploring different cultures, being in nature and trying different foods from all over the world are my favourite things to do. Travelling solo, as well as with family and friends, has been a great experience for me.
