Hi! I'm Evelyn

A young Mexican living in Canada, specializing in Digital Enterprise Management at the University of Toronto. After moving to Canada to study in 2015 and falling in love with the country, I stayed there ever since.

Among my favourite hobbies are travelling, photography, and art. Travelling is something I've been doing since I was 11 years old, and I enjoy learning about different cultures while doing it. The beauty of nature is something I'm passionate about and therefore I enjoy taking photographs of it. I started doing digital illustrations without previous experience in 2017 to experiment with something new.
